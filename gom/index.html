Welcome to the mockup for wiz's GOM website! Here you will be able to see a rough idea of the different views and pages that will eventually be built out into a fully-functioning platform.

What this site is:

  • A rough draft
  • In desperate need of feedback
  • A vision of a future where we can all be saved from spreadsheet hell and trying to juggle forms/posts/notifications/DMs/emails

What this site isn't:

  • Functional in any way, shape, or form (despite appearances!) You can't register for an account or create GOs yet, and all the pages have been filled with test data
